Welcome

I am a senior year undergraduate student in the Computer Science Department at UCLA. I am also an undergraduate student researcher at Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy (VCLA) advised by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu and Prof. Tao Gao, and I work closely with Siyuan Qi (PhD student) there. My research interest lies in combining artificial intelligence and cognitive science, focusing in commensense reasoning and social strategy making using computational models.

My research objective is to build a human mind into machines. I am currently trying to close this gap by equipping machines with commonsense reasoning.
